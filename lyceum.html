Lyceum Background

Welcome to Lyceum Project, my private consultation project that distinguished offline/online discussion that converges in-depth discussions on data, mathematics, statistics and automation. The consultation tailored for any data practitioners especially those who lack a traditional data background from any industries. I recognize that the true meaning behind the numbers remains elusive, leaving us questioning the depth of our insights. Therefore, I aim to unravel the complexities of statistics, providing sessions to solving their problem by delve deeper and bridge the gap between formulaic calculations and meaningful interpretations.

By automating data processing, practitioners can channel their efforts into strategic thinking and creative exploration.

Lyceum serves as a safe space for individuals seeking to consult on their problem in project related to data. Not only that, I will help them enhance their statistical understanding, embrace automation, and ultimately unlock the full potential of their roles in the data landscape.
